Limu - Music Player

Limu is an application designed to provide a convenient and synchronized music listening experience from various sources on your iPhone. With Limu, you can access and play music from your iTunes library, Google Drive, Dropbox, and files on your iPhone easily and flexibly.

Main feature:

- Multi-Source Music Integration: Limu allows you to connect and synchronize from multiple sources. You can access any song in your iTunes library, Google Drive, Dropbox, and files stored directly on your iPhone

- User-Friendly and Easy-to-Use Interface: Limu's user interface is designed to be simple and intuitive. You can easily search, filter, create playlists, and play your favorite songs with just a few taps

- Offline Music Listening: Limu enables you to download MP3 files stored on Google Drive, Dropbox, add music from iTunes, Files so you can listen anytime without needing an internet connection

With Limu, listening to music from various sources becomes simpler and more convenient than ever before. Experience it today and explore the diverse world of music on your iPhone!
